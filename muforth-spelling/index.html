MuFORTH spelling

Depending on how I’m feeling, I generally write it as one of the following:

  • muforth
  • muFORTH

The latter is more in keeping with Forth’s history. Forth was orignally spelled FORTH. The popular public-domain Forth that got me interested in Forth was spelled fig-FORTH (see fig-FORTH). And Chuck Moore's first commercial Forth product was spelled polyFORTH (see polyFORTH).
